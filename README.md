# PongSkin

## This project is an experiment on polish. Several techiques were tried to add flavour to the traditional pong game

### Camera shake
This is the most influentaial of the polish techniques of them all, take alone none of them have a great influence onf the polish feel than the camera shake. It was also added last in the project in a slighlty exagerated way for the sake of experimentation.
I would say that for any game mechanic that has any form of "violent" impact or collision, camera shake is a must!

### Light show
Controlling the environment light has a great impact on the mood of the game. Used right it can make a difference between blissful and depressing, depending on the design intention. While localized lights that feature movements colours and contrasts are usefull in directing the players eyes to where the developers intended. 

### Animation
In toays worls animation come for granted especially in high quality games. It seems that having animation alone would not make anything feel special in the game. however, not having animations of the apropriate quality would definetly break the metaphor. While certain gametography techniques can be used to distract the attention of the player from the animations, they are still an essential polish instrument.

### Environment
Being able to tell the player what is happening without saying or writing anything is the job of the environment, by placing the character in the middle of somewhere the environment can tell the player that this is how the world looks like, this is whats missing and whats plentifull. what seems theatning is probably an enemy, what seems dark an norrow is probably dangerous, what is high and lit up is probably the objective.
